      <li>Ma libi switi anga taawan. Teke tyali ati fasi luku taawan. Teke paadon gi taawan enke fa Masaa Gadu du anga yuseefi sondee i be fiti dati. Bika na a wooko fu Kelestesi namo meke a du dati.</li>
